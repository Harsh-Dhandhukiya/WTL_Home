About Us

Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU) is a premier university located in Gandhinagar, Gujarat. It offers a wide range of programs in energy studies, engineering, management, and humanities.

Course Offered

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)
  • Master of Technology (M.Tech)
  • B.A/ B.B.A/ B.Com
  • Ph.D. Programs

Admissions

Admissions to PDEU are based on merit and entrance examinations. Visit the official website for details on the admission process, eligibility criteria, and important dates.
